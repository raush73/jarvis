# Jarvis Modules — MW4H v1 (Single-tenant)

## A) CRM + Sales
- Company/Contact profiles
- SIC/NAICS markers + calling-day classification markers (FRIDAY-related)
- Call notes + call recordings stored at contact level
- Auto-dialer entry point + auto-scheduled follow-ups

## B) Order Management
- Create order → email customer for review/approval
- Customer edits/adds notes/approves
- Status changes to: **Needs to Be Filled**
- Recruiting dashboard always shows all open needs

## C) Recruiting + Candidate Management
- Candidate profiles (skills, tools checklist, PPE checklist)
- Availability status (Active seeking / Not active)
- Candidate recommendations when order hits **Needs to Be Filled** (marker-based ranking)

## D) Dispatch + Safety Compliance (Mandatory)
- Pre-dispatch checklist: orientation + general safety + site-specific videos
- Text links to worker phone
- Quiz/verification questions
- Dispatch locked until checklist completed + consent captured
- Daily safety video via text for active field employees (completion required before day dispatch)

## E) Time & Hours
- Weekly hours tied directly to orders/assignments
- Preferred: customer enters hours directly into Jarvis
- Export weekly hours file compatible with QuickBooks
- Auto-send export to accounting

## F) HR Employee History
- Full employee history
- All projects/customers/orders associated with each worker
- Safety records: violations, near misses, recordables (time-stamped, tied to job/customer/order)

## G) Customer Portal
- Customer login
- Real-time order status
- (Later) hour entry + approvals

## H) Accounting Integration
- Weekly hours compilation
- QuickBooks export workflow
- Alerts for missing/late hours

## Licensing later (future)
- Multi-tenant partitioning plan (Org/Account boundary)
- Tenant-specific permissions, templates, integrations
