## Roles (initial)
- Sales
- Recruiting
- Ops/Dispatch
- Management
- Accounting
- Customer (portal)
- Worker/Candidate (portal)

## High-level access rules
- Sales: companies/contacts, calls/notes, create leads, create draft orders
- Recruiting: candidate profiles, matching, fill orders, view safety summary
- Ops/Dispatch: dispatch workflow, compliance completion, assignment tracking
- Management: full visibility, reporting, configuration
- Accounting: hours review, exports, billing reports
- Customer: their orders + status, (later) hours entry
- Worker: profile, availability, compliance videos, check-ins

## Consent & audit requirements
- Consent disclosures are explicit and stored at employee profile level
- Every dispatch lock decision is auditable (who/when/what requirement blocked)
- No continuous tracking; only point-in-time verification with consent
